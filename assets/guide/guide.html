Strawvarie Hydration Hero

Create your custom pixel-art hydration buddy in a few steps.

What you need

  • A photo of yourself (or whoever should be the hero)
  • Google Gemini (free)
  • This Hydration Hero app

1 Get a pixel-art reference

Download a pixel character style you like from Magnific (free):

magnific.com/free-photos-vectors/pixel-character

Pick a simple standing character — this is the style reference for Gemini.

2 Gemini — Prompt 1 (create character)

Upload your photo and the pixel reference to Gemini. Paste this prompt:

Use my picture as the character and make it pixel art character like in the reference picture

Save the result image Gemini gives you.

3 Gemini — Prompt 2 (animate)

Upload the pixel character from Step 2. Paste this prompt:

Animate this character in strict 2D pixel art style. The character walks in from the left side, stops in the center, raises a metal tumbler to his mouth to take a drink, lowers it, turns around, and walks out to the right. Keep the character's appearance exactly the same. No camera movement, locked static frame. Maintain the solid magenta background perfectly flat with no shadows or lighting changes.

The flat magenta/pink background is important — the app removes it automatically.

4 Save your video

  1. Download the animation video from Gemini.
  2. Rename it exactly: hero.mp4

5 Copy into the hero folder

Move hero.mp4 into this folder on your Mac:

~/Strawvarie Hydration Hero/hero.mp4

In the app, click Open hero folder to open this location quickly.

6 Back in the app

  1. Once hero.mp4 is in the folder, Create my hero will unlock.
  2. Click Create my hero — we extract frames and build your character (about 1 minute).
  3. Click Preview reminder to see your hero in action.
  4. Scroll down in the app to adjust Reminder every (min) and Snooze (min).
  5. Click Minimize to dock — your hero will remind you in the background.

Video tips

  • 10–30 seconds is ideal
  • Solid magenta/pink/green background works best
  • Character should walk in → drink → walk out
  • Keep the camera still (no zoom or pan)
